About me

I like to design and develop complicated self-sustaining systems with new insights into previous problems. I have a strong passion towards making my designs as perfect as possible within the allotted project timeframe. Working with other meticulous minded and project focused engineers is a personal goal of mine, as I am focused on seeing creating progressively better designs and would like to work with like-minded people.
